How to Play

1. Start with any five-letter wordType a valid word and submit your first guess.
2. Check the color cluesGreen means the letter is in the correct spot. Yellow means the letter is in the word but in a different spot. Gray means the letter is not in the word.
3. Solve in six triesUse each clue carefully and narrow down the answer before your attempts run out.

Why Play?

Fast and simplePlay in your browser with no download required.
Mobile friendlyDesigned to look clean and load quickly on iPhone and Android.
Daily brain trainingPractice vocabulary, spelling, and pattern thinking.

Puzzle Tips

Begin with words that include common vowels and different consonants. Avoid repeating letters too early. After each guess, focus on confirmed green and yellow letters and remove gray letters from your next attempt.

Common letters such as A, E, R, S, T, L, and N can help reveal useful clues quickly.
